Methodology

FPS Formula (6 Components)
FPS = 0.35 × Wealth
      + 0.22 × NIL
      + 0.18 × Athletics Revenue
      + 0.10 × Athletics Fundraising
      + 0.07 × Endowment
      + 0.08 × (100 − Blended Risk)
All components use PERCENTRANK.INC normalization (0–100). Endowment is log-transformed before ranking to remove Texas $50B distortion. Higher score = higher percentile within all 138 schools.
Blended Risk
0.55 × Donor Concentration Risk (top-3 donors as % of total weighted score)
+ 0.25 × Industry Cyclicality (weighted-avg volatility of donor industries)
+ 0.20 × Blank Node Penalty (unfilled donor slots out of 16)
NIL Coordination (3 components)
40% Collective spend estimate (On3/CBS — journalism grade, not audited)
35% Recruiting rank signal (247Sports composite, 2025+2026 avg)
25% Revenue-share implementation status (House settlement, post Jul 2025)
